# Problem and Value Proposition

## The problem: Broken EdTech Math

Traditional interactive EdTech for classrooms is expensive and hard to scale:

- **~$2,000 per room** for proprietary interactive displays or smart boards.
- **Proprietary lock-in** — districts depend on vendors for updates and replacement parts.
- **Generates future e-waste** when hardware is retired or unsupported.
- **~$20,000 to equip just 10 classrooms** — financially crippling for many districts to scale.

*Scaling interactive technology to a whole school using traditional methods is financially broken.*

## Smart Pi value

Smart Pi Projector offers a cost-disruptive, repair-forward alternative:

- **~$215 per Starter Kit** (Smart Kit). Equip **10 classrooms for ~$2,150 instead of $20,000**.
- **Any flat wall becomes a touchscreen whiteboard** using IR tracking and a Raspberry Pi.
- **No vendor lock-in** — districts own their hardware. Open-source, community-maintained ecosystem.
- **Upcycling e-waste** — surplus/thrifted projectors and a Pi-powered brain extend useful life and support Right-to-Repair and sustainability goals.
