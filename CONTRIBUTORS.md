# Contributors

Thank you to everyone who contributes to Smart Pi Interactive Projection.

Contributors are also recognized in release notes (see [CHANGELOG.md](CHANGELOG.md)) and on the GitHub [Contributors](https://github.com/YOUR_ORG/rashbarrypi/graphs/contributors) page.

## Initial project

- Smart Pi Contributors (repository scaffold, pipeline, docs, and community setup).

To add your name: open a PR that adds yourself under a "Contributors" or "Additional contributors" section, or ensure your commits are associated with your GitHub account so you appear on the repo's Contributors graph.
